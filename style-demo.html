Design Principles

  • Subtle depth: Single inset shadow for recessed elements, minimal drop shadow for raised
  • Minimal gradients: Solid colors preferred, gradients only when necessary
  • Restrained effects: No glows, no multiple shadows, no heavy borders
  • Consistent lighting: Light source from top (inset highlight on top edge)
  • Fast transitions: 0.2s ease for snappy interactions
  • Muted colors: Lower saturation, focus on grays and subtle accents
